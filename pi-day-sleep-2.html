How the Pie Is Sliced
What an Optimal Night
Actually Looks Like
🔵
Light Sleep
~50%
Metabolic Regulation
The transition stage and baseline of your night. Makes up the bulk of total sleep and helps regulate metabolism.
🟣
Deep Sleep
~15-25%
Physical Recovery
Your body repairs tissue, builds bone & muscle, and strengthens the immune system during this phase.
🟢
REM Sleep
~20-25%
Mental Recovery
Memory consolidation, emotional processing, and cognitive repair all happen during REM cycles.
💡
If your Deep or REM slices are too small, you'll feel exhausted even after 8 full hours in bed.
