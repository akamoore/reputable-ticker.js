Roadmap

Implementation Phases

Two-phase rollout — Phase 1 infrastructure makes Phase 2 automation possible.

Phase 1: Minimum Viable

Now → 4 weeks
  • Data cleanup (CRITICAL) — Remove test/dummy accounts inflating numbers and hurting email deliverability. This is the #1 priority — accurate numbers and clean lists are the foundation for everything below. Establish naming conventions so they’re auto-excluded going forward.
  • Basic segmentation queries — Even manual list pulls would be a huge step. Minimum: “give me everyone not in an active study, not in washout, and has not tried Study X.” Multi-select filters across both in-app and lead databases.
  • Eligibility drop-off by question — Real-time monitoring so we catch problems within the first 10–20 people, not after 100+. Critical for every study launch.
  • Resolve Insufficient Baseline Bug — ~150 participants were stalled/lost due to data connection delays causing false “Insufficient Baseline” status. Fix in testing/release phase.
  • Dashboard spend accuracy — Ensure all campaigns mapped. Fix spend gaps. Implement 30-day vs all-time views.
  • Source attribution — UTMs and OneLinks are set up for every channel since January — data needs to flow into the dashboard. Add organic social & website tracking.
  • Manual queue visibility — For studies with off-app steps (labs, wearable setup), show Pending vs Completed status so we know exactly who’s stuck.
  • Document in-app vs outside-app touchpoints — Map every stage’s in-app and outside-app actions so the team operates from one coordinated playbook.
  • Basic email segmentation by lifecycle stage — Stop sending one blast to everyone. Segment by active, washout, available, churned at minimum.

Phase 2: Full System

4 wks → 3 months
  • Automated email campaigns — One-click segmented sends from dashboard. Trigger-based drip campaigns for each lifecycle stage (welcome series, washout engagement, re-enrollment prompts).
  • Push notifications & in-app messaging — Reach the "silent drop-off" people who installed but never joined. Nudge them toward studies they're eligible for.
  • Washout period tracking — Auto-track when washout starts and ends. Notify participants when they're eligible for new studies. Engage them with public studies in the interim.
  • Churn detection & re-engagement — Flag 3+ months inactivity. Auto-trigger re-engagement campaigns with incentives like bonus heartbeats.
  • Coordinated in-app + email drip campaigns — Unified sequences that span both channels at every lifecycle moment.
  • VIP tier system — Badges and exclusive content for participants who've completed 3+ studies. Targeted referral asks to high-value participants.
  • Compliance monitoring — At-risk flagging for active participants who stop completing tasks. Alert clinical team before it becomes a dropout.
  • Auto-exclusion rules — Once someone joins a study, automatically remove from all other recruitment campaigns. No more accidental double-recruitment.

Phase 1 infrastructure makes Phase 2 possible. The data cleanup, segmentation queries, and dashboard fixes are the foundation everything else builds on. We need Phase 1 right to unlock the full automation vision.
