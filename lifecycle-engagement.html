The Journey

9-Stage Participant Lifecycle

Click any stage to see in-app and outside-app touchpoints side by side.

1
Lead Capture
Ads, forms, referrals, organic
In-App
N/A — not in the app yet
Outside App
  • Lead capture from Meta Ads, Google, landing pages
  • Confirmation email: "Welcome to Reputable"
  • Referral links from existing participants
System Requirements
  • Auto-import leads from Meta Ads & forms into participant DB
  • UTM parameter tracking on all inbound links
  • Referral code generation & tracking
2
App Install
Download, attribution, first open
In-App
  • Onboarding screens & account creation
  • Prompt to enable push notifications
Outside App
  • "Thanks for downloading" email + quick start guide
  • Study code delivery if relevant
System Requirements
  • AppsFlyer SDK & install attribution
  • OneLink tracking & deferred deep linking
  • Install event triggers participant record creation
3
Browse / Explore
Study catalog, recommendations, nudges
In-App
  • Explore studies screen with filtering
  • "Recommended for you" nudges
  • Featured studies & in-app banners
Outside App
  • Email: "Top studies for you"
  • Push: "New studies you may qualify for"
  • Email digests of available studies
System Requirements
  • Browse activity tracking (views, clicks, time)
  • Push notification service (segmented)
  • Personalized study recommendation engine
4
Eligibility Survey
In-app screening with drop-off tracking
In-App
  • Eligibility questionnaire with progress bar
  • Partial save & resume
  • Real-time eligibility feedback
Outside App
  • Reminder email/SMS if they dropped off
  • Push nudge to finish survey
System Requirements
  • Question-level drop-off tracking
  • Disqualification reason logging
  • Real-time monitoring dashboard
5
Onboarding
Join study, labs, wearable setup, manual steps
In-App
  • Onboarding checklist with step tracking
  • Status indicator for pending manual steps
  • Scheduling links for labs/calls
Outside App
  • Email with lab kit / wearable instructions
  • Reminders: "You haven't completed your lab yet"
  • SMS follow-ups for time-sensitive steps
System Requirements
  • Manual queue tracking (pending vs completed)
  • Onboarding timeout alerts for stalled participants
  • Wearable device pairing verification
6
Active in Study
Daily tasks, compliance, rewards tracking
In-App
  • Study home with daily tasks & progress
  • In-app reminders & reward/heartbeat display
  • At-risk flagging for low compliance
Outside App
  • Occasional check-in emails
  • Educational content related to study
  • Minimal outreach to avoid noise
System Requirements
  • Real-time compliance scoring engine
  • At-risk participant flagging & alerts
  • Auto-exclude from recruitment campaigns while active
7
Study Completion
Exit survey, rewards, next-study suggestions
In-App
  • Completion screen with congrats animation
  • Points/heartbeats awarded
  • Next-study suggestions based on profile
Outside App
  • "Thank you" email + study summary
  • Invite to next relevant studies
  • Community content & success stories
System Requirements
  • Auto-trigger exit survey then washout period
  • Reward distribution engine
  • Next-study recommendation engine
8
Washout Period
Waiting period, public study nudges, washout end
In-App
  • Banner: "Washout until [date]" with countdown
  • Recommend allowed public studies
  • Auto-notification when washout ends
Outside App
  • Educational content & community emails
  • NO conflicting clinical study invites
  • Curated study email when washout ends
System Requirements
  • Auto-track washout dates per participant
  • Exclude from clinical recruitment during washout
  • Washout expiry triggers & eligibility re-check
9
Re-engagement
Churn detection, VIP recognition, email-only leads
In-App
  • "We miss you" card on next open
  • VIP badges & tier recognition
  • Early access to new studies for top participants
Outside App
  • Re-engagement: "Come back & earn X heartbeats"
  • VIP newsletters & referral asks
  • Email-only leads: app install pushes with value props
System Requirements
  • Churn detection (3+ months inactivity)
  • Engagement scoring & VIP tier system
  • Email-only segment & app install conversion tracking
  • A/B testing for re-engagement messages
↻

After washout or re-engagement, participants loop back to Stage 3

The lifecycle is continuous — participants cycle through multiple studies over time.

Roadmap

Implementation Phases

Two-phase rollout updated for the two-channel model.

Phase 1
Minimum Viable
Now → 4 weeks
  • Data cleanup of test/dummy accounts
  • Basic segmentation queries (manual pull)
  • Study-level dumps for active recruitment
  • Eligibility drop-off visibility by question
  • Dashboard fixes (spend, attribution)
  • Document in-app vs outside-app touchpoints per stage
  • Manual queue visibility for labs/wearable steps
  • Basic email segmentation by lifecycle stage
Phase 2
Full System
4 weeks → 3 months
  • Automated segmentation dashboard
  • One-click email campaigns from dashboard
  • Washout auto-tracking & participant status
  • API integration to email platform
  • Automated in-app notifications at lifecycle moments
  • Coordinated in-app + email drip campaigns
  • VIP tier system with badges & targeted emails
  • Churn detection with auto re-engagement (both channels)
