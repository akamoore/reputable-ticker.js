The Journey

12-Stage Participant Lifecycle

Click any stage to see in-app touchpoints, outside-app touchpoints, and system requirements side by side.

1
First Contact (Ad / Form / Website)
Lead capture from ads, forms, and organic sources
In-App
N/A — participant is not in the app yet
Outside App
  • Lead capture from Meta Ads, Google Ads, landing pages
  • Confirmation email: "Welcome to the Reputable ecosystem"
  • Referral links from existing participants
System Requirements
  • Auto-import leads from Meta Ads, landing page forms → participant database
  • UTM parameter tracking on all inbound links
  • Referral code generation & tracking
2
App Download
Install attribution, onboarding screens, notification opt-in
In-App
  • Basic onboarding screens
  • Prompt to enable push notifications
  • Account creation flow
Outside App
  • "Thanks for downloading" email + quick start guide
  • Study code delivery if relevant
  • OneLink deep link tracking from original ad/email
System Requirements
  • AppsFlyer install attribution & SDK integration
  • OneLink tracking & deferred deep linking
  • Install event → participant record creation
3
Browsing Studies, No Enrollment
Explore screen, personalized nudges, study recommendations
In-App
  • Explore studies screen with filtering
  • "Recommended for you" nudges
  • Featured studies & in-app banners
Outside App
  • Periodic email: "Top studies for you"
  • "New studies you may qualify for" push notifications
  • Email digests of available studies
System Requirements
  • Browse activity tracking (views, clicks, time on study)
  • Push notification service (segmented sends)
  • Personalized study recommendation engine
4
Eligibility Survey
In-app eligibility flow with drop-off tracking
In-App
  • In-app eligibility questionnaire with progress indicators
  • Partial save & resume capability
  • Real-time eligibility feedback
Outside App
  • Reminder email/SMS if they dropped off mid-survey
  • Push notification nudge to finish survey
System Requirements
  • Question-level drop-off tracking
  • Real-time monitoring dashboard
  • Partial save & resume capability
5
Enrolled / Active in Study
Daily tasks, compliance monitoring, in-app reminders
In-App
  • Study home with daily tasks
  • In-app reminders & progress indicators
  • Reward/heartbeat tracking display
Outside App
  • Occasional check-in emails
  • Educational content related to their study
  • Expectations setting (minimal to avoid noise)
System Requirements
  • Compliance scoring engine
  • At-risk participant flagging & alerts
  • Auto-exclude from recruitment campaigns while active
6
Manual / Off-App Steps
Labs, phone calls, wearable setup — steps outside the app
In-App
  • Status indicator: manual step pending or completed
  • Checklist view of off-app requirements
  • Scheduling links for labs/calls
Outside App
  • Clear instructions email with scheduling links
  • Reminders: "You haven't completed your lab yet"
  • SMS follow-ups for time-sensitive steps
System Requirements
  • Manual queue tracking (pending vs completed)
  • Onboarding timeout alerts for stalled participants
  • Lab kit shipment & wearable pairing tracking
7
Study Completion
Congrats, rewards, exit survey, next-study suggestions
In-App
  • Completion screen with congrats animation
  • Points/heartbeats awarded & displayed
  • Next-study suggestions based on profile
Outside App
  • "Thank you" email + study summary
  • Invite to next relevant studies
  • Community content & success stories
System Requirements
  • Auto-trigger exit survey → washout period
  • Reward distribution engine
  • Next-study recommendation engine
8
Washout Period
Waiting period between clinical studies
In-App
  • Banner: "You are in washout until [date]"
  • Recommend allowed public (non-clinical) studies
  • Washout countdown display
Outside App
  • Occasional educational content emails
  • Community engagement content
  • NO conflicting clinical study invites
System Requirements
  • Auto-track washout dates per participant
  • Exclude from clinical trial recruitment during washout
  • Public study catalog (non-restricted content)
9
Washout End
Washout expires, re-eligible for clinical studies
In-App
  • Automatic notification: "Your washout is over"
  • "Explore these studies" personalized card
  • Study catalog unlocked for clinical trials
Outside App
  • Email with curated study suggestions based on history & profile
  • Push notification: "You're eligible again!"
System Requirements
  • Washout expiry triggers (automated)
  • Eligibility re-check on washout end
  • Personalized study matching engine
10
Inactive / Churned
No login for 3+ months — re-engagement campaigns
In-App
  • On next app open: "We miss you" card
  • Easy re-engagement CTA with incentive
  • Personalized study recommendations on return
Outside App
  • Re-engagement email: "Come back and earn X heartbeats"
  • Incentive offers via email & SMS
  • Push notification re-engagement sequences
System Requirements
  • Churn detection (inactivity thresholds: 3+ months)
  • Segmented re-engagement campaign builder
  • A/B testing for re-engagement messages
11
Highly Engaged "VIP" Participants
Badges, tier recognition, referral program, exclusive access
In-App
  • Badges & tier recognition in profile
  • Early access to new studies
  • Special VIP flags & UI treatment
Outside App
  • VIP newsletters with exclusive content
  • Referral asks with extra incentive offers
  • Exclusive study invitations via email
System Requirements
  • Engagement scoring engine
  • Tier system (e.g. 3+ studies = VIP)
  • Referral tracking & VIP segment auto-tagging
12
Never Downloaded App (Email-Only Leads)
Leads captured but never installed the app
In-App
N/A — they have never downloaded the app
Outside App
  • Main channel: newsletters & study invites
  • App install pushes with clear value propositions
  • Targeted "here's what you're missing" email campaigns
System Requirements
  • Separate email-only segment in database
  • App install conversion tracking
  • Targeted value proposition messaging & A/B testing
↻

Stage 9 (Washout End) → loops back to Stage 3 (Browsing)

Participants cycle through multiple studies over time.
↻

Stage 10 (Churned) → can loop back to Stage 3 if re-engaged

Successful re-engagement brings participants back to browse studies.
↓

Stage 12 (Never Downloaded) → flows into Stage 2 (App Download)

Email-only leads convert when they finally install the app.

Roadmap

Implementation Phases

A two-phase rollout updated for the two-channel engagement model.

Phase 1
Minimum Viable
Now → 4 weeks
  • Data cleanup of test/dummy accounts
  • Naming convention for test accounts
  • Get data into their system
  • Basic segmentation queries (manual pull)
  • Study-level dumps for active recruitment
  • Eligibility drop-off visibility by question
  • Dashboard fixes (spend, source attribution)
  • Document all in-app vs outside-app touchpoints per stage
  • Manual queue visibility for studies with labs/wearable steps
  • Basic email segmentation by lifecycle stage
Phase 2
Full System
4 weeks → 3 months
  • Automated segmentation dashboard with filters
  • One-click email campaigns from dashboard
  • In-app notifications and drip campaigns
  • Washout period auto-tracking
  • Full participant status tracking
  • Churn detection and re-engagement flows
  • API integration to email platform
  • Automated in-app notifications at key lifecycle moments
  • In-app + email drip campaigns as coordinated pairs
  • VIP tier system with in-app badges & targeted emails
  • Churn detection with automatic re-engagement triggers (both channels)
