The Problem

Internal testing
is great for marketing.
It's not science.

😴
"I felt more energetic"
Subjective self-report. No way to verify if sleep, HRV, or recovery actually improved.
🍒
"9 out of 10 people loved it"
Cherry-picked results. The brand controls which data points get highlighted.
📝
"Clinically tested"
No independent oversight. The brand designed, ran, and interpreted its own study.
⚠️
The bottom line
When a brand runs its own testing, results are almost always subjective and unverified. The most innovative brands are choosing third-party, real-world data instead.
