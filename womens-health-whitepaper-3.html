Key Findings
How Diseases
Manifest Differently
in Women
🫀
Cardiovascular Disease
#1 killer of women
Atypical symptoms in women — like fatigue, nausea, and jaw pain — lead to delayed diagnoses and worse outcomes compared to men.
🛡️
Autoimmune Conditions
~80% of patients are women
Women's immune systems are more reactive, making them disproportionately affected by conditions like lupus, MS, and rheumatoid arthritis.
🧠
Mental Health
2x risk of depression
Hormonal fluctuations across the lifespan — puberty, pregnancy, menopause — uniquely impact women's mental health risk profiles.
These aren't edge cases — they represent fundamental biological differences that demand dedicated research.
