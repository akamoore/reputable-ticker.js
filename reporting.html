Berkeley Life: Missing entirely → $2,632.02 recovered
Berkeley Life was not displayed on the previous dashboard. Its $2,632.02 in ad spend was likely mislabeled as "Unmapped Campaigns" in the ads platform. 70 joined / 70 onboarded confirmed. Prescreening happened outside of the app.
Total gap: ~$4,200 previously unaccounted
